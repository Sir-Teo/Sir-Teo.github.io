NBA Prediction of Season 16-17

The original .ipynb file can be found here

I am a great fan of NBA, and I'm on Cleveland Cavaliers. (Although they can't even make to the finals this year). So I made a simple model to calculate some important data from NBA, including social influence, some important correlations and some graphs. Since I don't know how to collect data from nba.com , so I downloaded the sample data from fourthrityeight.com, you can do that!

This following image is one of the outputs, it shows that Lebron James is still the number one in the league, with the highest salaries, endorsements,franchise value, Minute per game. As you can see from this graph, different color areas represents different "heats", which tells numbers of players in the same level.

click here to view the raw output of the first example

Another interesting example is the "NBA Player correlation 'heatmap'" This map shows the relationship of different data of a player; for example, let's look at one interesting relationship (find blocks that are dark, they have a very close to 1 correlation coefficient): a player that has a high free thorw percentage(FT%) will also have a high field goal(FG%), this is true because the block that points to FG% and FT% has a dark color, which shows large correlaiton coefficient. There are more interesting data on this picture to explore! :)
